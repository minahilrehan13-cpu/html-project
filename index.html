Why I Love Photography

Photography is more than just pointing a camera at something — it is an art form that teaches you to truly see the world around you. Every shadow, every beam of light, and every fleeting moment becomes a story worth telling.

I picked up my first camera at the age of fourteen, and since then I have never put it down. What started as a simple curiosity grew into a deep passion that shapes how I experience everyday life. Whether I am shooting golden-hour landscapes or candid street scenes, photography keeps me present and inspired.

Essential Photography Gear

You do not need expensive equipment to start, but having the right tools certainly helps. Below are the essentials every beginner photographer should know about:

  • Camera body – a mirrorless or DSLR camera to get started
  • Prime lens (50mm) – versatile and great in low light
  • Tripod – for sharp long-exposure and landscape shots
  • Memory cards – always carry backups
  • Editing software – Lightroom or Darktable for post-processing
